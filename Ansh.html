Welcome to Ayodhya

Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Rama, is a city of great religious and historical significance.

History

Ayodhya is one of the seven sacred cities for Hindus. It is mentioned in the epic Ramayana as the birthplace of Lord Rama, a revered deity.
