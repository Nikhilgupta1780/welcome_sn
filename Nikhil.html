Welcome to Siddharth Nagar

Siddharth Nagar, a district in Uttar Pradesh, is renowned for its rich history, Buddhist heritage, and cultural significance.

History

Siddharth Nagar is named after Prince Siddhartha, who later became Gautam Buddha. It is believed to be the birthplace of Buddha and is steeped in Buddhist traditions.

Notable Places

  • Kushinagar: A prominent Buddhist pilgrimage site.
  • Kapilvastu: The ancient kingdom of King Suddhodana.
  • Lotus Temple: A serene and sacred location for tourists.
