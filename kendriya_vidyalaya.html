About Kendriya Vidyalaya

Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are a chain of schools run by the Government of India under the Ministry of Education. These schools are known for providing quality education with a focus on all-round development, discipline, and extracurricular activities. Our mission is to cater to the educational needs of children of transferable central government employees, including Defence and Paramilitary personnel.

Admission Information

Admissions to Kendriya Vidyalayas are based on the availability of seats and follow a set procedure. Parents can fill out an online application form during the admission period, which usually takes place every year. The school admits students from Class 1 to Class 12 in a variety of streams.

For more details, visit the official Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan Website.

Courses Offered

  • Primary Section: Classes 1 to 5 (General Subjects)
  • Secondary Section: Classes 6 to 10 (Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Languages)
  • Senior Secondary Section: Classes 11 and 12 (Science, Commerce, Arts)

Facilities at Kendriya Vidyalaya

Our school offers a variety of modern facilities to enhance the learning experience:

  • Spacious classrooms with multimedia support
  • Well-equipped science, computer, and language laboratories
  • Sports facilities including football, cricket, and athletics
  • Library with a vast collection of books
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Transport services

Faculty & Staff

Our dedicated and experienced faculty members are committed to providing quality education to all students. Each teacher is qualified and trained to teach their respective subjects and provide individual attention to the students. We also have non-teaching staff who help in maintaining a smooth functioning of the school.
